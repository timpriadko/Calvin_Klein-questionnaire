CK Unisex boasts a vegan formula containing how many naturally derived ingredients?
Unisex

CK Unisex is the new fragrance from Calvin Klein, opening a new chapter in the history of CK ONE which was one of the first unisex fragrance to gain wide popularity in the US.

CK Unisex symbolizes the spirit of the new generation, freedom of expression, the spirit of inclusivity and participation in multiple communities. A pure, green, citrusy scent, CK Unisex is announced as ‘a genderless scent for today that celebrates the infinite freedom of self-expression’.
