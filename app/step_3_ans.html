CK Everyone boasts a vegan formula containing how many naturally derived ingredients?
79%

CK Everyone boasts a vegan formula containing 79% naturally-derived ingredients. It is formulated with naturally derived alcohol, is vegan, recyclable, and comes in a folding carton that contains 30% post-consumer recycled materials. The citrus-themed scent includes notes of organic orange oil, blue tea accord and cedarwood.
